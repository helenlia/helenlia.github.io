Projects

  • The Legend of Zelda (Unity Remaster)

A remake of the first dungeon in The Legend of Zelda NES game. Features all rooms, enemies, and core mechanics. A custom level featuring "Simon Says" puzzle mechanics can be accessed by pressing "4".

Contributions: Implemented all player weapons (including sword, bow, boomerang, and bombs) and enemy item drops and collectables. Programmed player and enemy health and damage scripts, including knockback. Developed custom level with unique puzzle mechanics. Designed and implemented UI elements including health display, item inventory, and alternate weapon display. Also implemented animated doors, sound effects, pushable blocks and old man room.

Technologies: C#, Unity

  • Hueflip

Player traverses through the map and solves puzzles by toggling between a red map and blue map.

Contributions: This was a solo project, so I implemented all game mechanics, including player movement, color toggling, and puzzle elements such as color-switching platforms, toggled platforms, and pushable blocks. Background assets were created by Trixie, sound effects are from CoffeeValenBat, and music is from Brackeys. All other sprites were created by me.

Technologies: C#, Unity, Piskel
